Hard Services

Every built environment, whether it’s a school, an office or a block of flats requires certain ‘hard’ services.

They are the day-to-day. The essential core elements that allow your business to function properly. At Oracle Managed Services we pride ourselves on our expert integration of people, places, processes and technology to better enable the tireless servicing of those facilities.

Our comprehensive and professional range of hard facilities management services is supported by our extensively experienced network of regional UK offices and local resources. And it is by building our reactive maintenance services around this nationwide nexus that we can consistently offer a superior and affordable solution to all your hard facilities management needs.

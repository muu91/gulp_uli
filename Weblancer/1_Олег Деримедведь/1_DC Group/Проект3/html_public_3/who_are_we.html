Oracle Managed Services is an integral part of the DC Group, adding another service level and dimension to client satisfaction, by offering a complete facilities management package.

WHO ARE WE?

We’re already a key support provider for a wide range of clients across many sectors, who save time, money and hassle by letting us deal with one or several of their responsibilities and requirements. We not only help blue-chip companies, government departments and large retail chains, but we also have a growing portfolio of small to medium-sized businesses, schools, colleges and charities.

All benefit from our focus on outstanding customer care with every service we provide. Regardless of your business or organisation’s size, or the extent of our involvement, we see each new project as an opportunity to build a lasting partnership with you, based on a sound understanding of your sector and your unique requirements.

We can only do this by ensuring we recruit and retain people of the highest quality to work with us. Our people and partners share and uphold our values and standards, so we can guarantee with confidence that the services we provide are of the highest quality, delivered on time and with minimum disruption.

OUR ACCREDITATIONS

We recognise that health and safety is paramount to our customers, that is why you can be safe in the knowledge that Oracle’s internal standard is also in line with the ‘Approved Safe Contractor’ accreditation as well.

Learn More

We are now Constructionline Gold approved. PAS 91 Mandatory (C1-C4) verified. As well as an extended verified PQQ (covering Environmental Management, Equalities and Diversity, and Quality Management, and ensuring legislative compliance).

Learn More

Oracle is now compliant with various ISO accreditations including 9001, 14001 & 18001. ISO 9001 means we have been able to consistently prove that our services’ meet our customers’ needs accurately. Our completion of ISO 14001 represents our continued dedication to environmental management and our internal commitment to measuring our Eco footprint.

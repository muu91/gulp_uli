  • How does Migo work?

    Migo shows you great ride options available nearby, and lets you choose the one best suited to your needs — and never any added fees! See why riders have used Migo over 1 million times - don’t miss out!

  • Find and compare rides right now

    We’ll show you how close available services are (walking or waiting) and how much they cost right now (just enter your destination).  Discover new services whether you’re near home or you’re traveling.

  • See unique info to help you decide

    We’ll include information to give you confidence to ride.  Health safety ratings show you the policies in place to protect your health.  Pickup reliability ratings show you how services actually perform in meeting the time estimates you’re seeing.

  • Ride with new services directly in Migo

    Found a new service that’s just right for you?  You can often sign up and ride with that service directly in Migo (skipping another app download)!  We make it easy for you to ride with all of the services around you, and we’re adding more options all the time.

  • Take any ride with no added fees

    We don’t add any fees to any of the prices you see in Migo.  Period.  So rest assured that using Migo is completely free and doesn’t affect the prices we show.

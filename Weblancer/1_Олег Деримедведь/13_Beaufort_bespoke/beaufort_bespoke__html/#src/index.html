Our Story

Beaufort Bespoke Kitchens is a family run business founded by brothers Jon and Bryan Sandell. Based in a modern workshop facility between Bristol and Bath we’ve earned an exceptional reputation for kitchen design and craftmanship. Having invested in the latest manufacturing equipment we combine technology with traditional cabinet making skills to provide our clients with bespoke kitchens and furniture that are second to none.

We are craftspeople, we are innovators, we are perfectionists and we have a passion for what we do. These values underpin every piece of furniture we create.

Frequently asked questions.

  • Are there any discounts available with AiScreen?

    Yes, there are discount incentives for paying in advance and annually, rather than monthly. You can review these discounts by toggling between your “Monthly” to “Annual” plan type using the toggles above. For any questions regarding education or non-profit discounts, please contact our team for more information.

  • What happens at the end of my free trial?

    At the end of your 14-day free trial you’ll need to add a payment method in order to keep access to your screens and the content you’ve created. Accounts without a payment method will be suspended, but don’t worry, you can enter your payment details at any time to reinstate your screens and content.

  • How are my payment details stored?

    We take security around payment details very seriously. That’s why we use Stripe – a well-known and highly trusted provider of payment services and an accredited PCI Level 1 Service Provider. Our servers never need to see your credit card information and we know it’s held safely.

  • Are any apps and integrations an extra cost to my subscription?

    Most of the apps available for use and listed at aiscreen.io/apps are free with your AiScreen subscription. There are some services which may require an additional cost to you, but you are provided with that information in advance and these are normally third-party websites that require you to sign up for their service. Our guides will also reflect if a service is a third-party website and has an additional cost. If you have any questions about a specific app or service, please contact hello@aiscreen.io.

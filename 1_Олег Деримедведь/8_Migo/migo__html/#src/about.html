Meet the Migo Team

Migo was launched in 2017 by a group of savvy travel tech experts who saw how the power of travel search could revolutionize the world of on-demand transportation (also known as Mobility as a Service â€” or MaaS). Our experience working with Kayak, Trivago, and TripAdvisor led us to understand how Migo could help people get from A to B more efficiently, cost-effectively, and, even better, help them discover exciting, fun, and helpful new options.

Migo recently launched in Paris, France but is also available in more than 75 cities in the U.S., Canada, and Europe, including Seattle, Portland, and Washington, D.C.
